A branding system developed for the United Nations Sustainable Development Solutions Network (UNSDSN), a global initiative which works with the UN, private sector, civil society, and leaders from all regions and diverse backgrounds to promote solutions that address the interconnected economic, social, and environmental challenges confronting the world.

The system uses the basic primary shapes common to all cultures and references the primary colors used by in De Stijl design theory to communicate cross-cultural unity and utopian harmony.
System Two

This version of the system uses math created by the mobious strip and References to math/science in connection to infinity and a self sustaining shape to communicate the UNSDSN's mission statment of learning and using science to promote sustainability and a higher quality of life for everyone.
